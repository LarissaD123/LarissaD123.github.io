About Me

Hello. My name is Larissa Dong and I am a Graduate of Media Deisgn School with a Bachelor of Creative Technologies.

I am the main rigger and animator of the Streaking Reindeers group and have made games such as Reigndeer and Hot Pot.

In my spare time I like to create and play games of all types from indie to AAA. My other hobbie include drawing, cooking and crocheting.

I am proficient in Photoshop, Maya, UE4, Unity and Paint Tool Sai. I also have experience with Zbrush, 3DSMax and Blender.

Contact me at larissa_dong@hotmail.com or call me at 021 1538910.
